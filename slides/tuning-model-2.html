Tuning Model

Given data $~x^j = (x^j_1, \ldots , x^j_N)$, weights $~w= (w_1, \ldots, w_N)$, and labels $~y_j$, let $$\sigma_{w}(x^j) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-w \cdot x^j}}.$$ Find a $w$ that minimizes $$- \frac{1}{n} \sum_{j = 1}^n \left( y_j \log{\sigma_w(x^j)} + (1 - y_j) \log{(1 - \sigma_w(x^j)} \right)$$
